The Department Objective

The Grounds and Gardens Service ...

  • Provides an environment which helps to enhance the University's academic, social and business activities.
  • Maintains the distinct character of the individual sites within the campus.
  • Retains, maintains and renews the varied specimen tree stock.
  • Promotes a variety of uses for the grounds.
  • Creates a safe environment for all students, staff and vistors.
  • Delivers the highest possible standards for open days to aid in student recruitment process.
