Apply Glysophate Weed Killer

This is a regular activity, performed during the growing season, scheduled every 4 weeks for all our sites. Application is by back pack pump up sprayer. The product applied is Glysophate based systemic.

Do's and Dont's

  • High Winds will cause spray drift and collateral plant damage.
  • Plants must be actively growing so ambient temperature must be above 5oC
  • Heavy or persistent rain will wash product off foliage before it is absorbed.

Requirements

  1. Glysophate Weed Killer x 5 Litre Tub
  2. Pesticide Spray Record Sheet.
  3. Pesticide Spray Suit.
  4. Sufficient Water Barrels.
  5. Spare Spray Tips.
  6. 1 Litre Clean Water Spray.

Pre Activity

If the sprayer has been previously used with a different product make sure that it is washed thoroughly first. The dilution ratio for spot spraying is 50:1. Add 200ml of concentrated product into the measuring phial located in the mouth of the spray pack. Add water to the spray tank until the 20 litre mark has been reached.

During Activity

Both hard areas and soft areas can be treated with this product. Be aware of drift as neighbouring plants may be affected. Do not over apply. One drop kills.

Post Activity

Complete the Pesticide Spray Record sheet. Replace weed killer, spray suit and sprayer in chemical cupboard.
