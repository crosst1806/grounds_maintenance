Final Blow Up

This is usually the last activity performed on a site or an area, after all cutting has been completed. All leaves, twigs and other debris are to be blown off paths, driveways, car parks et cetera so that hard areas are left clean and tidy.

Do's and Dont's

  1. Do not direct the blower towards any nearby people.

Requirements

  1. Petrol Blower
  2. Petroil Mix Fuel

Pre Activity

During Activity

Always be aware of the whereabouts of fellow workers and others. Be careful not to blow dust or debris onto those nearby. Pay particular attention at corners and hidden entrances.

Post Activity
